IMPORTANT NOTES

  • Check spelling of all emails and teams (this is how the algorithm sorts--double check for typos)
  • Use ONLY lowercase letters for emails and teams
  • The Applicants_Ranking_Teams sheet, Teams_Ranking_Applicants sheet, and Team_Places sheet should each be in a separate .xlsx file (make 3 separate Google Sheets documents, one for each sheet and only that sheet)
  • Alternates should be listed in Teams_Ranking_Applicants sheet. The Team_Places sheet will take care of the # of spots filled ultimately (i.e. teams should list all applicants top to bottom, no need to distinguish alternates besides listing the # of spots available in Team_Places
  • This website will also notify the client which applicants did not match
  • Only applicants that are ranked by a team will be potentially accepted to a team
  • Only teams ranked by an applicant will be a potential match for the applicant
